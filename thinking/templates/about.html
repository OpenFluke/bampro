About BAMPRO

BAMPRO (Biofoundry And MARL Paragon Repo Orchestrator) is an open, modular research platform designed to advance multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL) at scale, with full transparency and real-time experiment control.

The Experiment

  • Multi-Agent RL at Scale:
    • Run large-scale MARL experiments in simulated worlds with dynamic agent spawning, competitive/cooperative tasks, and evolving environments.
  • Paragon AI Framework:
    • Modular, numeric-type agnostic neural networks with support for standard, replay, and dynamic replay training modes.
    • Analyze performance across architectures and ablation studies—all in one pipeline.
  • Biofoundry Simulation:
    • Procedurally generated planets, cubes, and resource nodes provide a rich playground for emergent agent behaviors.
  • Live Orchestration & Dashboard:
    • Start, stop, and monitor experiments in real time via a modern web interface.
    • Admins can control experiments with a password; anyone can view logs and results instantly.
  • Reproducibility:
    • Everything runs via Docker Compose for instant, repeatable setup—making your science easy to share and build upon.

Why BAMPRO?

  • Democratize cutting-edge AI experimentation by making it open, transparent, and interactive.
  • Enable researchers, students, and developers to design, test, and visualize new agent architectures and learning strategies with minimal friction.
  • Promote reproducibility and community-driven improvements through open source code and modular design.
  • Accelerate research on multi-agent systems, emergent behaviors, and real-world-inspired AI.
“One command to science: launch, orchestrate, and visualize MARL experiments from anywhere.”
